NORMAL BMI

Maintaining a normal BMI is a significant achievement that reflects a healthy balance between weight and height. To further enhance your well-being, focusing on a well-rounded workout, diet, and nutrition routine is essential. Engage in a combination of cardiovascular exercises, strength training, and flexibility exercises to promote overall fitness and muscle tone. Prioritize a diet rich in nutrient-dense foods such as lean proteins, whole grains, fruits, vegetables, and healthy fats. Stay hydrated and control portion sizes to ensure you're fueling your body optimally. Listen to your body's cues of hunger and fullness, and aim for consistency rather than extreme measures. Remember, your goal is not just weight maintenance but overall vitality and health. Consulting with a healthcare professional or a registered dietitian can provide personalized guidance tailored to your needs and goals.

NUTRITION This Plan is Crafted according to you BMI

MEAL

Aim for three main meals (breakfast, lunch, and dinner) and two to three small snacks throughout the day.
Each meal should ideally include a balance of carbohydrates, proteins, and healthy fats.

MID-MORNING SNACK

Greek yogurt with a handful of mixed nuts.
A fruit like an apple or a banana.

HYDRATION

Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
Herbal teas, coconut water, and infused water are good choices.

TIPS

Minimize processed snacks, sugary drinks, and packaged foods.
Pay attention to portion sizes to prevent overeating.
Include sources of healthy fats like almonds, walnuts, and olive oil.
