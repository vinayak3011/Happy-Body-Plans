NUTRITION This Plan is Crafted according to you BMI

MEAL

Aim for three main meals (breakfast, lunch, and dinner) and two to three small snacks throughout the day.
Each meal should ideally include a balance of carbohydrates, proteins, and healthy fats.

MID-MORNING SNACK

Greek yogurt with a handful of mixed nuts.
A fruit like an apple or a banana.

HYDRATION

Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
Herbal teas, coconut water, and infused water are good choices.

TIPS

Minimize processed snacks, sugary drinks, and packaged foods.
Pay attention to portion sizes to prevent overeating.
Include sources of healthy fats like almonds, walnuts, and olive oil.
